About Afghanistan

Afghanistan is a landlocked country located in South Asia, bordered by Pakistan to the east and south, Iran to the west, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, and Tajikistan to the north, and China to the northeast. It has a rich history and diverse culture, with breathtaking landscapes ranging from mountains to deserts. Afghanistan is known for its unique architecture, traditional music, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality.
